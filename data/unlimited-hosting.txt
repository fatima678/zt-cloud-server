FAQS
What Shared Hosting Really is?
It is allocated space for a site with a virtualized set of resources, obtained from actual servers. In shared hosting, many websites are hosted on a single server.
Because of sharing, the package is affordable for the majority of website owners, especially small business owners. In fact, it is the most popular option.
What are the advantages of shared hosting?
The very first advantage is its very low price, so the startups and small business may not need to overthink when they need web hosting.
You can upgrade our package easily when your requirements increase.
It has a built-in cPanel to manage your website without any hassle.
You do not need to take measures for technical maintenance since it is included in the package.
In short, it is well expandable, affordable, and easily manageable.
What is the difference between shared hosting and WordPress Hosting?
Shared hosting can be used for any content management system such as Joomla, Drupal, and the like including WordPress.
WordPress hosting is exclusively customized and optimized to make WordPress work at optimum.
What is the difference between shared hosting and VPS?
Since shared hosting has many websites on the same server, you have to share the resources with others. In shared hosting:
You get limited bandwidth.
You can’t perform some administrative tasks independently.
It is vulnerable to cyber-attacks.
VPS hosting is more expensive than shared hosting, so it comes with noticeable differences and advantages. In VPS hosting:
You can customize your options, as you have dedicated resources.
Your web hosting performs better.
You have a greater bandwidth allocation.
It has a stronger security.
